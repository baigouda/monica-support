隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 5 月

概述

莫妮卡背单词（以下简称"本应用"）尊重并保护用户隐私。本应用不收集、存储或传输任何个人信息。

数据存储

本应用的所有数据（包括学习进度、宠物状态、设置偏好等）均存储在您的设备本地，不会上传至任何服务器。

iCloud 同步

本应用使用 Apple iCloud Key-Value Store 在您的设备之间同步学习进度。此功能完全通过 Apple 的 iCloud 基础设施运行，开发者无法访问您的 iCloud 数据。您可以在系统设置中关闭 iCloud 同步。

第三方服务

本应用不使用任何第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。

应用内购买

本应用通过 Apple App Store 提供应用内购买。相关交易由 Apple 处理，本应用不会获取您的支付信息。

儿童隐私

本应用不会有意收集任何年龄段用户的个人信息。

联系方式

如有任何隐私相关问题，请通过 App Store 页面联系开发者。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Monica Vocabulary ("the App") respects and protects user privacy. The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Data Storage

All data in the App (including learning progress, pet status, and preferences) is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

iCloud Sync

The App uses Apple iCloud Key-Value Store to sync learning progress across your devices. This operates entirely through Apple's iCloud infrastructure. The developer has no access to your iCloud data. You can disable iCloud sync in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases through the Apple App Store. All transactions are processed by Apple. The App does not access your payment information.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from users of any age.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact the developer through the App Store page.
